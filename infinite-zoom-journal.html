Entry I  ·  The Beginning
✦
"There is a universe
inside every quiet moment,
waiting to be written."
This is not a journal you read from top to bottom.
You read it by falling inward — deeper, further, closer
to the centre of something true.

Entry II  ·  Memory
◈
"Memory does not live
in the past — it lives
in the texture of now."
I remember the smell of rain on warm stone.
Not a specific rain — every rain that ever was.
The body keeps its own library, ink-stained and infinite.

Entry III  ·  Growth
⟁
"Every scar is a root.
Every root is reaching
toward some invisible light."
Growth is not a straight line upward.
It is a tangle, a spiral, a return.
You arrive at the same clearing seven times — each time, taller.
✿   Seasons of the self   ✿
Entry IV  ·  Loss
☽
"Grief is love with
nowhere to go — so it
builds a home inside you."
I have carried names that no one else will say again.
I have kept them warm by speaking them in private,
in the rooms of myself that never sleep.
⌘   The things we keep   ⌘
Entry V  ·  Wonder
✧
"The sky is not a ceiling.
It is a mirror — and you
are what it reflects."
I lay on the grass and let the cosmos look back.
There was no gap between me and the dark.
Only a long, slow breathing — shared.
∞   The edge of the known   ∞
Entry VI  ·  The Centre
◉
"You have reached the centre
of the spiral —
and the centre is you,
exactly as you are."
Here, at the bottom of falling inward,
there is only stillness and a single truth:
every page you read was always about here,
and here was always waiting for you.
